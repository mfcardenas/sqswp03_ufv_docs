Laboratory Process

A structured 3-phase approach combining theoretical foundations with hands-on practice and real-world application.

Phase 1: Preparation (3 hours)

Required Reading: ISO 9241-11:2018 sections on usability definitions and context of use

Comparative Analysis: Evaluate major travel platforms (Booking.com, Airbnb, Skyscanner)

Comprehension Quiz: Validate understanding with 70% minimum score

Phase 2: In-Lab Session (2 hours)

TravelEase Case Study: Analyze a comprehensive tourism platform connecting travelers with local experiences

Team Collaboration: Work in rotating roles to ensure comprehensive skill development

AI Integration: Use ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini to create detailed user personas

Phase 3: Technical Report (3-4 hours)

Professional Documentation: 8-12 page technical report following industry standards

Evidence-Based Analysis: Support conclusions with concrete evidence from user research

Actionable Insights: Translate user context into design implications and measurable criteria
